Executive Summary

A New Kind of
Quantitative Fund

Ganbatte Capital deploys a diversified portfolio of quantitative trading strategies across multiple asset classes, instruments, and timeframes — designed so that no single strategy, market, or condition determines the fund's performance.

Core Principle
Smoother Equity Curve

Multiple uncorrelated strategies balance each other — when one draws down, others compensate. By combining strategies with different risk profiles and return drivers, the portfolio produces a smoother equity curve than any individual strategy could achieve alone.

Design Philosophy
No Single-Strategy Risk

The fund avoids dependence on one instrument, one timeframe, or one market condition. Strategies are continuously researched, validated, and deployed based on where the strongest risk-adjusted opportunities exist.

Capital Management
Adaptive Allocation

Capital shifts dynamically to strategies showing the strongest current edge. Built by former leaders from elite proprietary trading firms, the platform spans equity index derivatives, commodities, currencies, digital assets, and individual equities.

Industry Validation
Proven Model

The world's most successful quantitative firms — Citadel, Jane Street, Two Sigma — all run multi-strategy portfolios. This approach is why they consistently generate exceptional risk-adjusted returns across all market conditions.

FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is the minimum investment?
$250,000 provides meaningful participation and aligns with the fund's target investor profile.
How quickly can I withdraw?
After a 12-month lock-up, withdrawals are quarterly with 15 days' notice.
What if the market experiences extreme volatility?
Multiple strategies are designed for different conditions. Adaptive controls reduce exposure automatically. Many of our strategies close positions intraday, reducing overnight exposure. The multi-strategy design means the portfolio is not fully exposed to one scenario.
Is the fund tied to one strategy or market?
No. We deploy multiple strategies across options, futures, equities, and digital assets. New strategies are continuously added. No single strategy, instrument, or timeframe defines the fund.
How do I track my investment?
Regular performance reports will be provided to all investors with full transparency on strategy-level and portfolio-level results.
